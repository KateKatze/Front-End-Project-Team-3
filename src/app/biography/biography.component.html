Biography

Maximilian Romako

My name is Maximilian Romako, i am a professional photographer and I love to take pictures, especially of people, in every kind of way.


Even though i was born behind the iron courtain, in East Berlin, i am acutally an Englishman, having spent most of my youth in Sussex, before i went to study photography and fine arts at the London Imperial College. Now I live in Vienna, where i met my wonderful wife Ida. I often travel for work, always looking forward to finding new places and meeting new people.


Since I was a small child, i ve always had a keen interest in photography, how a camera is constructed, how it works and how pictures are taken was always a fascinating marvel to me!


When taking pictures of people, my goal is to show what is underneath: beauty, grace, strength, a persons energy and enthusiasm. If possible, i focus on non-models, putting regular people in exaggerated situations to highlight aspects who they truly are.


As i ve mentioned earlier, I am based in Vienna but frequently travel through Europe and the world for projects and work. Occasionally, i also assist other photographers, as i consider it a wonderful opportunity to grow further and find new inspiration. I do a lot of studio photos, along with portraits, landscapes, the art of dancing as well as weddings. I cooporate with professional agencies around the world and was fortunate enough to gain a lot of experience already. Feel free to have a look at some of my certificates and awards below, to get a more in depth understanding of my qualifications as well as my journey in the art of photography.


I hope you enjoy my photography. If so, and if you would like to work with me, please use my contact page or various social media channels to leave a message. I am always eager to make new experiences and meet new people!
